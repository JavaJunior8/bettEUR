Find your way around our digital campus

Educational Resources

Here, users can find comprehensive links to Academic Resources, including online course materials, e-libraries, and learning management systems. This section is vital for students and faculty, offering direct access to essential academic tools and databases, facilitating seamless learning and research activities.

Accessibility Quick Tools

This section provides immediate access to a range of Accessibility Tools tailored for users with different needs. It includes options for text-to-speech functionality, screen reader compatibility, and adjustable display settings like text size, contrast, and light/dark mode. This area is a gateway to making the website more accessible and user-friendly, particularly for those with visual or auditory impairments.

Contact Directory and Helpdesk

The Contact Directory and Helpdesk serves as a one-stop resource for all university contacts. It includes links to various departments, technical support, and specifically, disability support services. This centralised directory makes it easier for users to find the right contact information and get assistance promptly.

Interactive Assistance

Featuring an AI-powered Chatbot and a comprehensive FAQ section, this box offers instant support and guidance. The chatbot is designed to answer common queries efficiently, while the FAQs cover a broad range of topics, ensuring that users can quickly find answers to their questions.

Campus Information

This section provides detailed information about the Campus Facilities, including maps, accessibility features, student services, and accommodation details. It's an essential resource for new and current students, helping them navigate the campus and access various services easily.

News and Events

Stay updated with the latest happenings at the university through the News and Events section. This includes announcements, news articles, and a calendar of upcoming events, keeping the university community informed about academic schedules, seminars, workshops, and other significant events.

Research and Innovation

The Research and Innovation box showcases the university's latest research projects, publications, and innovative ideas. It serves as a platform for students and faculty to explore and engage with the university's academic advancements and research opportunities.

Career and Opportunities

This area focuses on Career and Opportunities, offering resources for career development, job listings, internships, and professional growth. It's a critical tool for students and alumni looking to advance their careers and find meaningful employment and collaboration opportunities.

Community and Networking

The Community and Networking box provides platforms for community engagement, including fora, alumni networks, and social media links. It fosters a sense of community and offers networking opportunities for students, staff, and alumni to connect and collaborate.
